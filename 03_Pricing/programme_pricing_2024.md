# Programme Pricing Structure — 2024

## Day Rates (Minkowski Team)

| Role | Standard Rate | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Senior Futurist / Programme Lead | €4,000–€4,500/day | Sarah Chen tier |
| Facilitator / Coach | €2,800–€3,500/day | Marc de Vries tier |
| Junior Facilitator / Associate | €1,500–€2,000/day | |
| Programme Design & Prep | 50–75% of delivery rate | Per prep day |

## Programme Fee Logic

### Small programme (1–2 days, <30 participants)
- Design + delivery: €18,000–€35,000
- Typical team: 1 lead + 1 support

### Mid-size programme (2–3 days, 30–80 participants)
- Design + delivery: €45,000–€90,000
- Typical team: 1 lead + 2–3 facilitators

### Large programme (2.5–4 days, 80–150 participants)
- Design + delivery: €120,000–€200,000
- Typical team: 2 seniors + 3–5 facilitators

## Modular Pricing Add-ons
- Futures scan / signal research: €8,000–€15,000
- Scenario development (custom): €15,000–€25,000
- Follow-on coaching (per leader, 3 sessions): €3,500
- Digital toolkit or workbook design: €4,000–€8,000

## Commercial Notes
- Venue and logistics always passed through at cost, not marked up
- International travel at cost
- Day rate discounts possible for multi-programme agreements (>3 programmes/year): 10–15%
- Avoid fixed-price proposals for highly custom or poorly scoped briefs — use day rates with capped ceiling instead

## Exceptions & Flags
- Rabobank 2024: programme fee structured as flat fee due to budget cycle; margin was tight (28%)
- Target margin: 35–45% on programme fees
- Below 25%: escalate before committing
