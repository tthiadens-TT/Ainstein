# Worked Example — Mid-Market Leadership Programme (€60k)

*Seed content generated 2026-04-18. Adapt before client use. Supersedes nothing.*

## Context
Anonymised composite. Mid-market European industrial company, €1.2B revenue, new CEO 9 months in. Brief: "help our top 30 leaders become more future-ready ahead of the 2027 strategy cycle."

## Brief profile (against the decision matrix)
- **Client profile:** Mid-market corporate.
- **Brief complexity:** Medium — multi-module, one stakeholder group, one discipline focus.
- **Team shape:** Standard — 1 Senior Futurist + 1 Facilitator/Coach + 1 SME.
- **Indicative tier:** Mid, lower half of the band.

## Programme shape
Three modules over 10 weeks.

1. **Module 1 — Futures Sprint (2 days, residential)** — see Part 3B in `02_Tools/02_Tools_Agent_README.md`.
2. **Module 2 — Scenario deep-dive (1 day, office)** — expert-led, focused on one industry-specific scenario.
3. **Module 3 — Activation review (half day, office)** — leadership move pressure-test, 90-day plan.

Plus: pre-programme interviews (8 × 45 min) and a closing written synthesis memo for the CEO.

| Line | Basis | Amount |

| Travel & venue coordination | Flat (venue billed separately) | €1,500 |
| **Subtotal** | | **€60,700** |
| Rounded client price | | **€60,000** |

Venue and catering billed separately at cost — see `05_Venues/`.

## Margin read
- Direct cost (fees to faculty + materials + travel): ~€34,000.
- Gross margin: ~43%. Clears the 35% floor comfortably.

## Where this could flex
- **Remove SME module** → price drops to ~€52k, margin holds. Use only when scenario work is genuinely generic.
- **Add 1:1 coaching for top 6 leaders** → +€18k at standard coaching rates (see `programme_pricing_2024.md`).
- **Compress to 1-day Futures Sprint** → price drops to ~€42k; not recommended for a top-30 group.

## What made the pricing hold
- The essence statement was clean: "prepare the top 30 to pressure-test the 2027 strategy."
- Three modules mapped directly to three decisions.
- No custom collateral beyond the closing memo.
- One buyer, clear sign-off.

## What would have broken the pricing
- Adding "a short podcast series" or "a digital toolkit" without recovering cost.
- Accepting procurement's request to include quarterly check-ins for 12 months at no extra fee.
- Agreeing to deliver in three languages without a translation line.
